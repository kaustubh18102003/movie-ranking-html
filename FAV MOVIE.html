    In the title role as the reanimated mummy.
    The film follows adventurer Rick O'Connell as he travels to Hamunaptra, the City of the Dead.

    Development took years, with multiple screenplays and directors attached.

    The crew endured dehydration, sandstorms and snakes shooting on location in the Sahara Desert.
    Industrial Light & Magic provided many of the visual effects, blending live-action footage and computer-generated imagery to create the titular monster.
